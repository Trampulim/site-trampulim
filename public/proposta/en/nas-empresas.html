Corporate flagship Featured

Pratubatê

Imagine an event where every team member is not a mere spectator, but the protagonist of an unforgettable experience. It’s not a lecture. It’s not a show. It’s a human connection tool disguised as a celebration.

PRATUBATÊ is an interactive show that uses music and drums as a communication tool. It was developed for companies and institutions seeking to evoke a sense of teamwork, cooperation, partnership and unity. The company is like an orchestra — each department has its own tuning and potential, yet all work together towards a common goal.

Suddenly, every participant has a drum in their hands. Initial scepticism gives way to curiosity. One person’s timid sound starts to join their neighbour’s. Within minutes, what was noise becomes rhythm. Hierarchies dissolve. The whole room vibrates with a powerful, unison energy — living proof that, together, we are capable of creating something grand.
