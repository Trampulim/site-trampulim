Interventions
& Acts

Smaller acts that fit situations beyond the stage — ideal for parties, festivals and events. Each act is a miniature with a beginning, middle and end, condensing all the group’s precision and energy into a few minutes.

Benedita Jacarandá & Sabonete

A Receita

Benedita is a chef full of culinary inventions. With the (dubious) help of her assistant Sabonete, she tries to follow a recipe to the end. A comic scene of precise timing and stage complicity.

Photography: Agência Viewtiful · Rafael Protzner · Flávia Mafra · André Fossati · Netum Lima

Adriana Morales

Bexiga Imperativa

Turns a traumatic personal experience into raw material for humour. The performance works on vulnerability, ridicule and the courage to laugh at oneself as an act of healing and resistance.

Photography: André Fossati

Tiago Mafra

Rotina

A solo creation that puts a clown face to face with the crushing weight of everyday life. The clown, who lives on hope and affection, confronts the irony of an ever-heavier routine.

Photography: André Fossati · Bruno Vinelli

Benedita Jacarandá

Flerte e Dance

A dance-and-clown solo inspired by the classic "Flashdance". Benedita faces the conflict between her desire to dance and the limitations of her own body. Choreography, physical comedy and vulnerability.

Photography: Lina Mintz · Grupo Trampulim

Adriana Morales

Partida — Short Scene

A short-scene version of the show Partida. Benedita Jacarandá flees her creator in a poetic investigation into identity and fractured relationships. Solo by Adriana Morales.

Photography: Adrilene Muradas · Tamás Bodolay
