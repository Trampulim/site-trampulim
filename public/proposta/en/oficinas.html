Workshops

Being a clown is a state that requires a specific logic. Grupo Trampulim’s workshops teach that language through scenic games, offering tools for artistic projects or personal development.

01Introduction

Percurso Afetado

An introduction to clown that dismantles preconceptions and equips artists with essential tools for scenic work. The clown state requires a specific logic — this workshop teaches that language through scenic games, practices, training, research and reflection.

Audience: Actors, performers, circus and performing arts students at any level.

02Street intervention

Invention of the Invasion

Workshop for up to 30 artists. Participants receive practical tools to create improvised street interventions in everyday spaces. It covers the relationship with public space, the passer-by as involuntary accomplice and the art of presence in the unexpected.

Audience: Circus, street and theatre artists interested in urban intervention.

03Theatrical improvisation

Trampulimpro Workshop

The combination of clown and theatrical improvisation as a practice for expanding scenic presence. The workshop emphasises physical exercises, spontaneity and comic expression, exploring what happens when the unexpected becomes an opportunity for creation.

Audience: Performing arts professionals, theatre students and circus artists.
