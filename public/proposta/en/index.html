The Group

Art that moves,
circus that speaks.

Founded in 1994 in Belo Horizonte, Grupo Trampulim has spent three decades building its own language across circus, theatre and music — always with artistic rigour and generosity towards the audience.

Winner of the PNAB 2025 Award, the group has performed at festivals in Brazil, Argentina, Uruguay and Europe.
